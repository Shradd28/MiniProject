--- OUR DANCE PROGRAMS ---

Ballet

Aiming to make ballet as a professional career? With only a small group class, Junior Ballet emphasizes a high level of personal teaching and close supervision.

Ballroom

Ballroom dance, type of social dancing, originally practiced in Europe and the United States, is performed by couples and follows prescribed patterns of steps.

Hip-Hop

Hip-hop dance refers to street dance styles primarily performed to hip-hop music. It includes a wide range of styles primarily breaking, locking, and popping.

Contemporary

Contemporary focuses on the freedom of movement. This allows dancers to explore emotions, and use their natural energy to portray personal expression in their dance techniques.

Salsa

Salsa is actually a blend of several Caribbean music and dance styles that uses simple harmony based on repeated chord patterns, often in the minor key.

Zumba

Zumba is a fitness program that incorporate interval training — alternating fast and slow rhythms — to help improve cardiovascular fitness.
